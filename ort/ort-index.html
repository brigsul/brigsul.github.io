The One Ring

Seven Ring Bearers over 4,860 Years

The one ring to rule them all. Created in the Second Age, destroyed in the Third Age.

Here's a time line of the Ring:

  • S.A. 1600 - The One Ring is forged
  • S.A. 1693 - The War of the Elves and Sauron Begins
  • S.A. 1697 - Eregion is destroyed and Khazad-dûm is closed
  • S.A. 1700 - The forces of Sauron are defeated by the Nûmenoreans and he retreats to Mordor
  • S.A. 3262 - Sauron allows himself to be taken prisoner by the Nûmenoreans
  • S.A. 3441 - Sauron is defeated, Isildur takes the One Ring
  • 4 October, T.A. 2 - Death of Isildur in the battle of Gladden Fields, The One Ring is lost in the River Andiun
  • c. T.A. 2463 - Dèagol discovers the One Ring and is slain by Smèagol who claims it for his own
  • T.A. 2939 - Saruman discovers Sauron's servants are searching Gladden Fields for the One Ring
  • T.A. 2941 - Bilbo Baggins finds the Ring in Gollum's cave
  • T.A. 2944 - Gollum leaves the Misty Mountains and begins searching for the Ring
  • September 22, T.A. 3001 - Bilbo freely gives up the Ring and it passes to Frodo Baggins

The War of the Ring Begins

  • T.A. 3018 - Gandalf finds a scroll written by Isildur describing the One Ring in Minas Tirith. Gollum is captured by Sauron and, under torture, reveals the names Shire and Baggins
  • 23 March, T.A. 3018 - Gandalf questions Gollum about the Ring in Mirkwood
  • 13 April, T.A. 3018 - Gandalf holds the Ring briefly before throwing it into Frodo's fire and thus revealing its inscription and confirming its identity
  • 27 Septembery, T.A. 3018 - Tom Bombadil wears the Ring but appears unaffected by it
  • 25 October, T.A. 3018 - The Council of Elrond is held in Rivendell and it is declared the the Ring shall be destroyed
  • 25 December, T.A> 3018 - The Fellowship of the Ring sets out from Rivendell on their mission to destroy the One Ring in the fires of Mount Doom
  • 14 February, T.A. 3019 - Frodo offers the One Ring to Galadriel but she refuses to take it
  • 26 February, T.A. 3019 - Boromir attempts to take the Ring from Frodo by force. He dies shortly thereafter defending Merry and Pippin from Saruman's Orcs
  • 13-14 March, T.A. 3019 - Samwise Gamgee wears the Ring for about two days while Frodo is held captive by the Orcs in the Tower of Cirith Ungol
  • 25 March, T.A. 3019 - Sam and Frodo reach the Cracks of Doom in Mordor but Frodo refuses to destroy the Ring and claims it for himself. Gollum attacks Frodo and takes the Ring but falls into the fires of Mount Doom, destroying the Ring.

“For Isildur would not surrender it to Elrond and Círdan who stood by. They counselled him to cast it into the fire of Orodruin night at hand... But Isildur refused this counsel, saying: 'This I will have as weregild for my father's death, and my brother's. Was it not I that dealt the Enemy his death-blow?' And the Ring that he held seemed to him exceedingly fair to look on; and he would not suffer it to be destroyed.”

J.R.R. Tolkien, The Silmarillion
